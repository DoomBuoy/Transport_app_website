Your Journey with
Safarnama.

Google Maps shows you a plan. Safarnama shows you the reality. With 5-second live tracking, real-time car park counts, and cheap fuel finders, let the app do the heavy lifting while you enjoy the ride.

The Advantage

Everything you need to move.

We've expanded beyond simple routing. Discover specialized tools built for real NSW drivers and commuters.

Multi-Mode Tracking

Live GTFS positions for Metro, Trains, Buses, Ferries, and Light Rail, updated every 5 seconds.

Live Car Parks

Real-time availability for Commuter Park & Rides. Know exactly how many spots are left before you arrive.

Cheap Fuel Finder

Real-time fuel price comparisons. Instantly locate the absolute cheapest petrol, diesel, or E10 near you.

Live Stop Departures

Missed your bus? Pull up the real-time departure board for any stop to immediately see when the next ride is arriving.

Commercial & Loading

Specialized maps to find commercial loading zones in the CBD and high-clearance hubs for heavy-duty vehicles.

Highway Rest Areas

Driving long distances? Safarnama locates the nearest official highway rest areas so you can safely take a break.
