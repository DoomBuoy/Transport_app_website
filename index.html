Features

Everything you need to move.

We've stripped away the clutter and focused on the features that actually matter to NSW commuters.

Multi-Mode Tracking

Live GTFS positions for Metro, Trains, Buses, Ferries, and Light Rail. See exactly where your ride is on the map.

Live Car Parks

Real-time occupancy data with zone-level breakdowns. Check available spots and navigate directly to the entrance.

Smart Trip Planning

"Arrive By" logic handles complex routes. Special support for School Buses (Yellow) and On-Demand (Purple) services.

Specialized Markers

Tracking for Every Service.

We don't just show generic dots. Our map uses a smart color-coding system derived directly from the service type, so you know exactly what's approaching.

  • Yellow Markers for School Buses
  • Purple Markers for On-Demand & Coaches
  • Blue Markers for Standard Public Buses
  • Green Markers for Ferries
