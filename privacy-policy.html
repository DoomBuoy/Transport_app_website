Information Collection and Use

For a better experience while using our Service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information.

1. Location Data

The App requests access to your device's location data (Fine and Coarse location).

  • Why we need it: We use this data strictly to provide core functionality, such as finding your current location to calculate transit routes, showing nearby transport stops, and calculating distances to points of interest.
  • How it is handled: Your location data is processed in real-time and transmitted securely to third-party routing and mapping APIs (Transport for NSW, TomTom) to fetch transit directions.
  • Data Sharing & Storage: We do not store your location data on any external servers, nor do we sell or share your location history with advertising networks or third-party data brokers.

2. Local Device Storage

The App allows you to save "Home," "Work," and "Favorite" locations, as well as a history of "Recent Trips." This data is stored entirely locally on your device using local storage mechanisms (SharedPreferences). We do not have access to this data, and it is not uploaded to our servers. You can clear this data at any time by clearing the App's data in your device settings.

3. Analytics and Crash Reporting

We currently do not use any third-party analytics or crash tracking tools (such as Firebase Analytics or Crashlytics). We do not silently collect diagnostic data about your usage of the App.

4. Advertising

The App is currently ad-free and does not use third-party advertising networks. If we decide to introduce advertisements in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated to reflect the networks used and any associated data collection.

Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party services that may collect information used to identify you or to process your requests (such as mapping a route). These third-party service providers include:

  • Transport for NSW (TfNSW) Open Data API: Used for transit routes, alerts, and stops.
  • TomTom API: Used for location and point of interest (POI) searching.
  • OpenStreetMap (Nominatim): Used as a fallback for location searching.

We recommend reviewing the Privacy Policies of these third-party service providers to understand how they handle data transmitted via their APIs.
