How to install Safarnama

Because Safarnama is an independent student project, it isn't on the Google Play Store yet. Your Android phone will show a standard security prompt. Here is exactly how to bypass it safely.

Standard Android Security Prompts

Android devices flag all direct APK downloads with permission requests and a "Play Protect" warning. Don't worryâ€”this is completely normal for beta apps. Safarnama is 100% safe, ad-free, and respects your privacy.

1

Go to "Install unknown apps"

Before you can install the file, your phone needs permission. Go to your device Settings, search for Special access, and tap on Install unknown apps.

2

Allow Your Browser

Find the browser you used to download Safarnama (such as Chrome or Samsung Internet) in the list and toggle the switch to ON. This allows your browser to run the installer.

3

Click the downloaded APK

Navigate to your Downloads folder in your file manager or browser. Tap on the Safarnama.apk file to initiate the installation process.

4

Tap "More details"

When the Google Play Protect screen appears saying "App scan recommended", do not click Scan App or Don't Install. Instead, tap the small "More details" dropdown arrow.

5

Tap "Install without scanning"

Once the menu expands, you will see a hidden button appear.

Tap on "Install without scanning". The installation will now complete successfully, and you can open Safarnama!
